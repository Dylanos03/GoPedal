Where can I pick up my Bike?

We have pick-up points in 4 cities across the UK.
Manchester -
You can pick up or drop off your bike at any of the drop of points:
- Manchester Piccadilly
- Manchester Victoria
- Manchester arndale
Norwich -
There are 3 pick up and drop of points in Norwich:
- Norwich train station
- Castle Quarter
- Chantry place.
Bristol -
There is 2 Pick up and drop off points in Bristol:
- Cabot Circus
- City center.
Brighton -
The Pick-up/Drop-off points in Brighton are:
- Brighton train station
- Brighton pier
- Churchill Square Green

Become a member today!

Gold Membership

This is our top membership. With this you will get access to a rental bike 24/7, removing the late night usage charge, and will not be charged extra for leaving the bike at another pick-up/drop-off point

Silver Membership

This is our second tier membership. This membership allows you access to 20 rental bikes usages a month for free, with a reduced charge of £1.50 for any extra uses


Bronze Membership

This is our Base membership. With this membership, you will have access to 10 rental bike usages a month for free, with a reduced charge of £2 for any extra uses
